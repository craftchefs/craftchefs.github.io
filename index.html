About us

CraftChefs is a platform for talents, arts worth sharing. We provide platforms for your talent. Started in 2015 as a discussion between few friends, and now We're on a mission to provide a platform of people's desire. Here they can what they want to do, Here they can show up there talent, art to the whole world.

CraftChefs is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek interest in any kind of art. We believe passionately in the power of ideas, creativity and talent to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world.

CraftChefs is owned by a nonprofit, nonpartisan foundation. Our agenda is to make great people more greater by providing them their desired platform.
